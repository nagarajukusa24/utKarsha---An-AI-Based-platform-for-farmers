About Us

JAI KISAN is an initiative taken forward to help farmers and provide them all types of support and encouragement to grow them both financially and end up with making profits.This modern era needs an equivalent approach to the needs of agriculture and increase its productivity. To increase the productivity,farmers need to be aware of whats going on their fingertips.To make this happen for real,JAI KISAN puts forward the act of pushing farmers to know and makes everything easy right from start of crop to knowing their product price in the market.

Why JAI KISAN?

    The features which are very notably helpful to farmers are:

  • Schemes: Farmers can know about various details regarding schemes provided by Central and State governments.This page will be very useful in terms of information regarding daily updates.

  • Minimum Sale price: In this section,Farmers can be aware of the prices of various crops grown by them.This can be very handy for them so that the price is known in advance and act accordingly.

  • Contact:Farmers often be in troubles regarding any type of problem with their crop.So,who to be contacted? This page says it all whom to contact specifically and rectify their problem.

  • Soil Health Card: In this page,Farmers can get their current soil condition namely soil health card from the respective authorities by selecting specific district and mandal.

  • Register/Login Farmers and agents can first register and then login to continue as authorised people. Then they can be able to view their dashboards which make them effective in knowing the requests and approvals.

    • JAI KISAN,a brand new initiative to put ahead our farmers is solely meant for farmers all over the nation.We believe in a motto: The country sleeps well,only when a farmer sleeps well.
